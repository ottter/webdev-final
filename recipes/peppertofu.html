    Instructions

    1. 1.

      Crispy Tofu: Preheat oven to 350° F (175° C). Lightly oil a large baking sheet. Gently press tofu pieces to release as much water as possible. Toss tofu with corn starch and soy sauce (add a bit of brown sugar for neat twist) and let sit for ~5min. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, flipping occasionally

    2. 2.

      Season, slice, dice and saute the onion and broccoli to desired size

    3. 3.

      Mix in the tofu and Sesame sauce and remove from hot pan after 15-20 seconds

    4. 4.

      Serve on rice or noodles
